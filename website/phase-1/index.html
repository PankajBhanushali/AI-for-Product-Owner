Phase 1: AI Foundations

Master the fundamentals of AI, LLMs, and responsible AI practices

⏱️ Duration: 3 weeks
📚 Content: 12-15 hours
🎯 Level: Beginner to Intermediate

🎯 Learning Objectives

By the end of Phase 1, you will be able to:

Understand AI Fundamentals

Know the difference between AI, ML, and Deep Learning, and understand how they evolved

Master LLM Concepts

Understand how transformers work and why LLMs are revolutionary in product development

Recognize Ethical Issues

Identify bias, fairness concerns, and privacy risks in AI systems

Evaluate AI Feasibility

Assess whether AI is the right solution for a given product problem

📋 Phase 1 Summary & Key Takeaways

Fundamentals ✓

  • Understand AI, ML, DL hierarchy
  • Know training vs. inference
  • Recognize AI limitations

LLMs ✓

  • How transformers work
  • Capabilities and limits of LLMs
  • Effective prompting techniques

Ethics ✓

  • Bias and fairness issues
  • Privacy and data concerns
  • Responsibility frameworks
