AI Governance Framework

1. Team Structure Options

How should you organize your AI teams?

Option 1: Centralized AI Center of Excellence

One dedicated AI team serving the entire organization. Good for: early stage, building expertise, standardizing tools.

Option 2: Distributed AI (AI in Every Team)

Each product team has AI expertise. Good for: mature organizations, cross-functional autonomy, fast iteration.

Option 3: Hub & Spoke Model

Central CoE + AI engineers in each team. Good for: balance of standardization + autonomy, scalable.

2. Key Roles & Responsibilities

Who does what in an AI operating model:

  • Chief AI Officer: Sets strategy, allocates budget, removes blockers
  • AI Ethics Board: Reviews risks, audits for bias, makes go/no-go decisions
  • Data Governance Committee: Manages data quality, compliance, privacy
  • AI Product Owners: Define use cases, prioritize, own outcomes
  • ML Engineers: Build and maintain models
  • Data Engineers: Build pipelines, ensure data quality
  • AI Ethicist: Reviews fairness, audit logs, documentation

3. Governance Workflows

From Idea to Production: Four Gates

Gate 1: Business Case Review

Does this align with strategy? Is ROI positive? Approval: Product leadership

Gate 2: Ethics & Risk Review

Fair? Safe? Compliant? Approval: Ethics board + legal

Gate 3: Technical Review

Is model performant? Data quality good? Infrastructure ready? Approval: ML leadership

Gate 4: Launch & Monitoring

All metrics green? Monitoring set up? Approval: Product owner

4. Connecting to Business Strategy

AI shouldn't exist in isolation. Link it to business outcomes:

1. Business Goals

What does the company want to achieve?

2. AI Initiatives

Which AI projects support those goals?

3. Success Metrics

How will we know if this AI project succeeded?

4. Allocation

Budget and talent go to highest-impact projects

  2. Define 5-7 key roles and responsibilities
  3. Design your 4-gate governance workflow
  4. Create an Ethics Review Checklist

  6. Draft a quarterly AI prioritization process
