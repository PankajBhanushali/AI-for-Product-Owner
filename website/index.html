How to Get Started

  1. Choose your path: Begin with Phase 1 for comprehensive learning, or skip ahead based on your experience level
  2. Follow the curriculum: Each week includes lessons, key concepts, and applications to your role
  3. Complete the labs: Use templates and tools to apply concepts to real projects
  4. Build your capstone: Develop an AI product initiative (weeks 19-20)
  5. Take the exam: Validate your knowledge with the comprehensive assessment
  6. Defend your work: Present your capstone to executive leadership
  7. Celebrate: Earn your Certified AI Product Owner credential! 🎓
