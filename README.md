# AI for Product Owner - Enterprise Certification Track

[![License: CC BY 4.0](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-CC%20BY%204.0-lightgrey.svg)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)
[![Live Site](https://img.shields.io/badge/Website-GitHub%20Pages-blue)](https://PankajBhanushali.github.io/AI-for-Product-Owner/)

A comprehensive 20-week curriculum designed for enterprise product owners, strategists, and decision-makers who want to master AI integration in product strategy and execution.

## 🎓 Course Overview

This course is structured into **7 phases** covering everything from AI fundamentals to scaling production systems. Each phase contains interactive labs, templates, and hands-on exercises designed for real-world application.

### Course Structure

| Phase | Weeks | Focus Area |
|-------|-------|-----------|
| **Phase 1: AI Foundations** | 1-3 | AI Literacy & Ethics |
| **Phase 2: AI Strategy** | 4-6 | Opportunity Mapping & Strategy |
| **Phase 3: Product Execution** | 7-10 | Design, UX, & Engineering |
| **Phase 4: Data & Model** | 11-13 | Data Strategy & Model Lifecycle |
| **Phase 5: Governance** | 14-16 | Risk Management & Compliance |
| **Phase 6: Scaling** | 17-18 | Experimentation & Cost Optimization |
| **Phase 7: Capstone** | 19-20 | Build & Executive Defense |

### 📚 What's Included

- **Curriculum**: 20 weeks of structured learning content
- **Labs**: AI backlog templates, cost calculators, risk registers, and testing matrices
- **Assessments**: Comprehensive certification exam
- **Templates**: Ready-to-use tools for product teams
- **Case Studies**: Real-world AI product examples

## 📖 Curriculum Phases

### Phase 1: AI Foundations (Weeks 1-3)
Learn AI fundamentals and ethical considerations
- Week 1: AI Literacy
- Week 2: LLM Behavior & Capabilities
- Week 3: Ethics & Responsible AI

### Phase 2: AI Strategy (Weeks 4-6)
Develop strategic AI initiatives
- Week 4: Opportunity Mapping
- Week 5: AI Product Strategy
- Week 6: AI Customer Lifecycle

### Phase 3: Product Execution (Weeks 7-10)
Execute AI products in practice
- Week 7: AI Assistant Design
- Week 8: AI UX Patterns
- Week 9: AI Backlog & Engineering
- Week 10: Prompt Engineering

### Phase 4: Data & Model (Weeks 11-13)
Master data and model management
- Week 11: Data Strategy
- Week 12: Model Lifecycle
- Week 13: AI Metrics & Evaluation

### Phase 5: Governance (Weeks 14-16)
Implement governance frameworks
- Week 14: AI Risk Taxonomy
- Week 15: AI Compliance
- Week 16: AI Operating Model

### Phase 6: Scaling (Weeks 17-18)
Scale AI across the organization
- Week 17: AI Experimentation
- Week 18: AI Scaling & Cost Optimization

### Phase 7: Capstone (Weeks 19-20)
Demonstrate mastery through capstone
- Week 19: Capstone Project Build
- Week 20: Executive Defense
